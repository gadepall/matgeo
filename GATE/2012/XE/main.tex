\item For the matrix 
$\vec{M} = \myvec{
1 & 4 & 5 \\ 
0 & 2 & 6 \\ 
0 & 0 & 3}
,$
consider the following statements
\begin{enumerate}[label=\Alph*:, start=16]
\item 3 is an eigenvalue of $\vec{M}$.  
\item \myvec{4 \\ 1 \\ 0}  is an eigenvector of $\vec{M}$.  
\item \myvec{4 \\ 2 \\ 0}  is an eigenvector of $\vec{M}$.  
\end{enumerate}
Which of the above statements are TRUE?
