\item Which one of the following matrices is singular?

\begin{multicols}{4}
\begin{enumerate}
    \item $\myvec{ 2 & 5 \\ 1 & 3 }$
    \item $\myvec{ 3 & 2 \\ 2 & 3 }$
    \item $\myvec{ 2 & 4 \\ 3 & 6 }$
    \item $\myvec{ 4 & 3 \\ 6 & 2 }$
\end{enumerate}
\end{multicols}
